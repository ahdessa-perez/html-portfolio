The Best Movies According to Ahdessa

My Top 3 movies of all-time

Grave of the Fireflies

Anime Movie that made me cry.
Grave of the Fireflies is based on the short story of the same name by novelist Akiyuki Nosaka, who lived through the bombing of Kobe while he was a child. He lost two sisters to malnutrition, and his adoptive father was also killed. Nosaka reportedly spent the rest of his life wrestling with guilt over their deaths.

Spirited Away

There is never a scene that feels like it was made only for children. The dialogue, the humor, and the characters all display a unique sense of realism and maturity that normally isn't present in animated movies, especially those made by Disney.

Howl's Moving Castle

Howl's Moving Castle is a brilliant flight of imaginative fantasy. The hand-drawn animation is visually spectacular and the imagery teeters between nightmarish horror and haunting beauty. There are powerful philosophical messages about pacifism, youth, beauty, love and compassion.
